Infographic Poster

As a computer science and engineering major, I aimed to address the growing concern of cybercrime in this piece, with the goal of raising awareness and educating about potential cyber threats.

Overview

This project was divided into several steps.
  • Initially, I conducted research on cybercrime, my chosen topic.
  • Using the gathered information, I refined the topic to ensure its effective presentation in the infographic.
  • Subsequently, I conducted design research, before diving into an iterative design development process.
