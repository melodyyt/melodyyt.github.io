AR Storybook is a web-based application aimed at introducing computational thinking skills to children in the PK age group.

The objective of this project is to advocate for utilizing technology in a manner that encourages an early introduction to skills applicable across various daily activities. AR components, which introduce a sense of reality, can be triggered through image targets scattered throughout a physcial storybook. Essentially, AR storybook represents a contemporary adaptation of traditional storytelling, seamlessly incorporating advancing technological features that are becoming increasily prevelant in society.

The Story

Meet the Characters

Bunny and Beaver - two best friends

The Plot

Bunny has invited Beaver over for dinner! Beaver lives in the pond and Bunny lives in the meadow. With the help of the user, through AR activites, help Beaver make his way to Bunny's house.

AR Activities

The first activity will cover algorithmic thinking. Users will be asked to place a series of instruction in the correct order. When placed in the correct spot, the corresponding platform will turn green. If not, it remains red.

Next steps:
Replace with actual instructions.
Add textual user feedback.

The next activity will cover pattern recognition. Users will be asked to select the object out of three options that matches the displayed one.

Next steps:
Introduct some feedback that indicates which object users have selected.
Improve randomization of objects
