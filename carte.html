UI design project

A conceptual application that allows users to choose dining destinations in a fun and interactive way.

Overview

For this project, my aim was to develop an application that assists individuals or groups in making dining decisions. Users would engage by swiping left or right to skip or select options, respectively. The results would then be revealed once the choices are narrowed down.

User Study

To shape the user interface effectively, I conducted a user study survey after conceptualizing the app's functionality. Two phrases stood out:

"fun and animated"

"sophisticated"

To achieve this style, my aim was to develop a sleek interface while integrating design elements such as animated or cartoonish icons and color schemes that reflect a more cheerful vibe. Additionally, I planned to present photographs in a more creative manner to enhance the overall user experience.
